The Market Place - The perfect place to meet post-match After the match, The Market Place becomes the perfect destination to meet up with friends and family for a post-match drink or snack. We shall continue to serve our street-style food and our large range of drink options well after the final whistle.

Cheese Room

The stadium will aslo have it's own dedicated cheese room as part of it's VIP experience, so it's perfect for all those cheese enthusiasts. It will have a selection of pristine cheese's from all over the world so the selcetion will be vast and exciting.

Tunnel Club

The new stadium will have Britain’s first glass-walled players’ tunnel for fans to watch teams line-up as they head from the dressing room to the pitch. The “Tunnel Club” suite and restaurant allows supporters to eat while watching the goings-on as stars such as Harry Kane and Dele Alli prepare for kick-off.

Inside the stadium

Screens

There are four giant HD screens, the largest in the UK, and a state-of-the-art sound system. This will ensure that we can deliver pre-match, half-time and post-match entertainment, highlights and replays to the highest possible standard. Every Season Ticket seat will be in view of a screen.

Stadium atmosphere

The design of the stadium bowl optimises the atmosphere created ensuring the Club maintains its tradition of having one of the most atmospheric homes in British sport.

Pitch closeness

The seating bowl is arranged to place you closer to the pitch than at any other comparable size stadium in the UK. The angle of the stands is set at up to 35 degrees and this, combined with the close proximity, will help connect you to the play.

What to expect

Upon visiting there are a range of services that visitors can enjoy.

Food on offer

There will be a great selection of food on offer, from pizzas to Thai food to English pies, burgers, chips plus a vegan menu. These foods will be available before, during and after the match too so you can enjoy food here througout the whole day.

Brewery

The microbrewery will be the source of craft beer which is served throughout the stadium. There will also be a dedicated Beavertown Tottenham Taproom which will be open for fans to visit at all Tottenham Hotspur domestic home fixtures.

Events

We shall continue to serve our street-style food and our large range of drink options well after the final whistle.A dedicated stage will play host to a programme of post-match entertainment including live music, Legends and DJ Sets.
